Meet the Budding Florist

Sallie Kennedy (aka The Budding Florist) has had a lifelong love of flowers as well as a dream to own her own business. After retiring from her teaching job of 30 years, she decided to make that dream a reality and started The Budding Florist in 2022.

Devoted to bringing the color and joy of flowers to her clients, Sallie hand-selects everything that goes into a Budding Florist creation. From wrist corsages for Senior Prom, to holiday centerpieces, she specialize in floral designs that are vibrant and unique.
